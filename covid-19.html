Covid-19 Policy

Our Priority is to make your Salon experience as safe as possible,
Before opening the salon will have A full deep clean,
Masks are a must,
On arrival at the salon there is hand sanitiser provided at the door for use both on arrival and leaving,
if you or any of your housefold feel ill or display any symptons of covid-19 please contact us as soon as possible.
Please do not come to the salon for your appointment, we will re-book.
We will be wearing protective PPE at all times,
All tools and equipment will be disinfected after every use for safety reasons.

Treatments

Following updated guidelines from the NHBF and insurance all colour treatments must have a patch test 48hrs prior to appointment
# these will need to be updated on any colour change or every 6-12 Months.

While you are attending the salon for appointments all government guidelines will be followed.
